Story

This show revolves around Gada family which consists of uneducated businessman, Jethalal Champaklal Gada, Jethalal's wife, Daya & their naughty son, Tipendra Gada living in Gokuldham Co-operative Society and covers topical issues which are socially relevant.

In case of problem and emergency, Jethalal Gada often rushes to Taarak Mehta to seek his help and guidance as he finds a philosopher in him. Jethalal calls his father, Champaklal Gada from village to keep an eye on his mischievous son, Tapu but the opposite happens and Bapuji joins hands with Tapu. This hilarious show will surely tickle your funny bone.
